Experiment to compare Marking and Radial menus

Pre-experiment:

Instruction:

In this study, we would like to study about how marking menu compares with radial menu in terms of performance. We want to analyze their speed, accuracy and whether they are user-friendly enough. Therefore, we need you to join in this experiment to do some trials to test these two kinds of techniques.

Here is the instructions of how to use these two kinds of menus:
### Marking Menu
- Popup: Left Mouse Down
- Select: Stroke to leaf node
- Reset: Release Mouse Down

### Radial Menu:
- Popup: Right Click
- Select: Left Click
- Reset: Right Click

In each trial, you will need to find out a target menu button with these two kinds of menus. Each time, the menu kind, levels of menu and size of menus may be different. These pieces of information can be found at the top bar of trial page and each time you find the target button, you should press done “next” button to do next trial. At the end of all trials, you should press the “done” button to generate the log report for your experiment.

The total experiment should be done within around 12 minutes. Thank you for your time and cooperation.
